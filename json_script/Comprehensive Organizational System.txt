📋 System Overview
Management Structure Pattern
Each major section follows the consistent pattern:

XX.00 Index - Master index and category overview
XX.01 Inbox - Temporary holding for new/unsorted items
XX.02 Work in progress - Active projects
XX.03 To-dos & checklists - Action items and tasks
XX.04 Bookmarks & URLs - Web resources and links
XX.05 Templates - Reusable templates and forms
XX.08 Someday - Future possibilities and ideas
XX.09 Archive - Completed or inactive items
Key Features
Johnny Decimal System: Hierarchical numbering for easy navigation
Consistent Structure: Every major category uses the same management pattern
Professional Integration: Seamlessly blends personal and professional workflows
Educational Focus: Supports curriculum development and student project management
Creative Pipeline: Clear separation between inputs (research/resources) and outputs (finished work)
Scalable Design: Can accommodate growth in any category
Educational Applications
This structure models effective organizational systems for:

Project-Based Learning: Clear phases from planning to completion
Understanding by Design: Logical progression from goals to assessment
Student Portfolio Management: Template for organizing creative work
Curriculum Development: Systematic approach to lesson planning and resource management
